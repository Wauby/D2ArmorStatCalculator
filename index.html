Gear Stat Calculator

This tool calculates builds using masterworked T5 armor. (left number is min targeted stat, right number is max targeted stat) (dm wauby on discord with any bugs or issues you find)

Step 1: Set Target Stats & Constraints

Step 2: Armor Configuration (Optional)

Force Custom Armor Piece Lock one armor piece with specific stats you define. (Useful for exotic class items)
Include Exotic Armor Option Allow one armor piece to be an exotic with T2 stats (30 primary, 20 secondary, 13 tertiary).

Step 3: Custom Fragment/Font Mods (Optional)

Enable Custom Fragment/Font Mods Add custom stat bonuses from fragments, font mods, or other sources.

Note: These bonuses are applied after all armor and regular mods are calculated. Use this for fragment mods, font mods, or any other stat bonuses not covered by regular armor mods.

Step 4: Select Standard Mods

You have a max of 5 mods.

Step 5: Enable Advanced Mods (Optional)

Enable Stat Tuning Mods Allows using up to 5 mods that shift stats (+5 to one, -5 to another) or add minor boosts (+1 to three non-primary stats).
